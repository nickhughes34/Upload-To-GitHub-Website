Frequently Asked Questions!

What Is The Regualar Size Of A Business Card?

Business card size in inches is: 3.5" x 2".

Business card size in mm is: 85 x 55 mm.

Business card size in Photoshop is: 1050 x 600 pixels.

How Do You Organize Business Cards?

We recommend using one of our business card holders, available in a variety of different shapes and styles.

How Thick Is A Business Card?

The higher the "pt" the thicker the card. For example, standard cards are 14pt thick, and our thickest card is 32pt.

How Quickly Will Delivery Be?

We have a variety of delivery options, including standard (1-2 weeks), priority (2-5 days) and express (1-2 days).

If I Add Special Finish Does It Apply To Both Sides?

The front of your card will have whatever special finish or texture you selected. The back will be a smooth surface, great for adding notes.
